Outside Sources

----------------------------------------------------------------------

In the making of this website, I utilized two APIs. The first one is the Google Maps Geocoding API, which allowed me to convert a given city, and state into geographical coordinates, in longitude and latitude. The second one I used was the GeoDB Cities API, where I inputted longitude and latitude coordinates, and gave a radius in miles, and it returned me a list of cities in that radius. I used this list to search through created Hackathons in a file, and output any Hackathons which city was located in the X mile radius.
